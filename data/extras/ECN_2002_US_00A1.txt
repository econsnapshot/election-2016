UNIVERSE DESCRIPTION
The universe of this file is all operating establishments with
one or more paid employees. This universe includes all
establishments classified in the North American Industry
Classification System (NAICS) Codes 21 through 813990.
DATA ITEMS
This file contains data on:

  Number of establishments
  Sales, receipts, revenue, shipments, or business done
  Annual payroll
  Total employment

GEOGRAPHIC COVERAGE
The data are shown at the U.S. level and by State, county,
place, and Metropolitan Area.
INDUSTRY DETAIL
The data are shown at the 2- through 6-digit NAICS code levels
for all sectors.
OTHER VARIABLES IDENTIFYING RECORDS
For the Wholesale sector, data are published for Total Wholesale
Trade and for Merchant Wholesalers. For the Services sectors, data
are published for All Establishments, as well as Taxable and Tax
Exempt Establishments.
RELEASE SCHEDULE
The data in this file correspond to Industry Series and
Geographic Area Series reports that will be released starting in
May 2004 and ending in June 2005.
RELATED DATA FILES
Economy-wide data on products and sales size are released in the
Economy-Wide Products Statistics (EC0200SL1) and Economy-Wide Size
Statistics (EC0200SS1) data files.
CORRESPONDENCE TO PDF REPORTS
These data are exactly the same as are shown in the PDF-format
Industry and Geographic Area Series reports. These reports are
available on the Census Bureau's Internet site at
www.census.gov.
DATA LIMITATIONS
There are no special limitations to these data.
FILE SEQUENCE
Data are presented in ascending Geography by NAICS Code.
